Northstar Construction, Inc. v. Pelham Properties, LLCSwimlane — Cal. Super. Ct., S.F. County · CGC-24-617205

Northstar, the general contractor on a $4.25M renovation/seismic retrofit at 1488 Folsom St., sues Pelham for unpaid Draws #15 and #16 ($954,800), foreclosure of mechanic's lien, and account stated. Pelham answers and counterclaims for breach of contract, negligent construction, and breach of warranty, alleging deficient seismic anchoring and rooftop-deck waterproofing defects with $1.42M in remediation costs. Parties dispute the date of substantial completion (Oct 22, 2023 vs. Jan 30, 2024) and whether the work conformed to spec.
