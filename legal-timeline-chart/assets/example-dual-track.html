Wells v. Drayton Holdings, Inc.Dual Track — E.D. Pa. · 2:23-cv-01882-MAB

FLSA retaliation and wrongful termination action. Wells, a Senior Operations Analyst, alleges Drayton fired her for complaining about unpaid overtime. On Drayton's Rule 56 motion, the court denied summary judgment on the FLSA retaliation and wrongful termination claims (finding a triable temporal-proximity issue tied to a disputed date when HR received Wells's complaint) and granted it on the implied contract claim. Trial pending.
