Halverson v. Smith Capital Partners — S.D. Cal. · 24-CV-00891

Partnership dispute arising from removal of managing partner Halverson. Deponent Gregory Smith testified the removal-discussion meeting occurred on Friday, May 3, 2024 with no scheduling emails, but contemporaneous emails (Ex. 4 and Ex. 7) show the meeting was scheduled May 7, confirmed May 8, and actually occurred Friday, May 10, 2024. Relevant to a motion in limine.
