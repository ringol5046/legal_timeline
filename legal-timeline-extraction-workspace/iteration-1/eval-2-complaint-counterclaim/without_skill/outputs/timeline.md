## Key disputed facts (live timeline conflicts)

- **Substantial-completion date.** Northstar: 2023-10-22 (Compl. ¶9). Pelham: not before 2024-01-30 (Answer ¶4).
- **Quality of work / compliance with plans.** Pelham alleges deviations from at least March 2023 onward, supported by Aug. 4, 2023 Wexford report and the Dec. 4, 2023 § 12.3 Notice. Northstar disputed the engineering findings on Aug. 25, 2023.
- **Validity of the Mechanic's Lien** (recorded 2023-12-18) — Pelham challenges under Cal. Civ. Code § 8416.
- **Net dollar position.** Northstar seeks ≥ $954,800; Pelham seeks ≥ $1,420,000 + disgorgement + lien discharge.
