Plaintiff Northstar Construction, Inc. ("Northstar") alleges as follows:

## PARTIES, JURISDICTION, AND VENUE

1. Plaintiff Northstar is a California corporation with its principal place of business in Oakland, California.

2. Defendant Pelham Properties, LLC ("Pelham") is a Delaware limited liability company doing business in San Francisco. Pelham is the owner of certain real property located at 1488 Folsom Street, San Francisco, California (the "Property").

## GENERAL ALLEGATIONS

4. On or about **June 15, 2022**, Northstar and Pelham entered into a written Construction Agreement (the "Contract") under which Northstar agreed to act as general contractor for the renovation and seismic retrofit of the Property. The Contract is attached as **Exhibit A**.

5. The total contract price was $4,250,000, payable through monthly progress draws.

6. Northstar mobilized on **August 1, 2022** and commenced demolition.

7. On **November 4, 2022**, Pelham issued Change Order No. 1, expanding the scope of work to include relocation of mechanical systems. The cost impact was agreed at $312,400. **Ex. B**.

8. On **February 28, 2023**, Pelham issued Change Order No. 2, adding a rooftop deck and related structural reinforcement. The cost impact was agreed at $487,800. **Ex. C**.

9. Northstar substantially completed the work on **October 22, 2023**, on which date a Notice of Substantial Completion was issued. **Ex. D**.

10. Throughout the project, Pelham timely paid progress draws through Draw #14, paid on **September 5, 2023**.

11. **Draw #15**, in the amount of $342,500, was submitted on **October 30, 2023**. Pelham refused to pay this draw despite proper documentation.

12. **Draw #16 (Final)**, in the amount of $612,300, was submitted on **December 1, 2023**, following completion of punch list items. Pelham refused to pay.

13. On **December 18, 2023**, Northstar recorded a Mechanic's Lien against the Property in the amount of $954,800. **Ex. E**.

14. On **January 9, 2024**, Northstar sent Pelham a 14-day notice of default and demand for payment. **Ex. F**. Pelham did not respond.

## FIRST CAUSE OF ACTION
### (Breach of Contract)

15. Northstar incorporates the foregoing allegations.

16. Pelham materially breached the Contract by failing to pay Draws #15 and #16 (Final), totaling $954,800.
