Wanke, Industrial v. AV Builder Corp. — Timeline-Anchored Case Map

Cal. Ct. App. 4th Dist., Div. 1 · D074392 · filed 2020-02-19 · Judgment affirmed
Wanke held a 2013 judgment against Keck and WP Solutions for $1,190,929. To collect, it filed a creditor's suit against third party AVB for the $109,327 AVB owed WP Solutions under five waterproofing subcontracts. The trial court awarded Wanke $83,418.94; the Court of Appeal affirmed on all three appellate issues.
