Wanke, Industrial, Commercial, Residential, Inc. v. AV Builder Corp.Centered Axis

Cal. Ct. App. 4th Dist., Div. 1 · D074392 (Super. Ct. No. 37-2016-00023774-CU-EN-CTL)

Wanke obtained a 2013 judgment against Keck and WP Solutions for $1,190,929. To collect, Wanke filed a creditor's suit against third party AVB seeking $109,327 AVB owed WP Solutions under five waterproofing subcontracts. After bench trial, the court entered judgment for Wanke for $83,418.94, largely rejecting AVB's setoff claims. The Court of Appeal affirmed on Feb. 19, 2020, rejecting AVB's standing/capacity, statute-of-limitations, and warranty-setoff challenges.
